Overview:

This task asks you to choose between an investment and a lottery. You will receive the bonus of {{Constants.high_bonus}} if your choice pays out and no bonus otherwise. The study ends with a very short survey that you also need to complete to be paid any bonus payments.

Task description:

There are 100 investment opportunities. We randomly pick one investment opportunity and offer it to you for free. Out of the 100 investments there are {{prior_g}} good ones. Suppose you take the investment, you will receive the bonus of {{Constants.high_bonus}} if the investment is good and no bonus if the investment is bad.

Before you decide whether to take the investment opportunity, we will also assign a consultant to help you inspect its quality. After the inspection, the consultant will report to you whether he thinks the investment is good or bad. The consultant is either Bob or Mike. Both Bob and Mike may make mistakes, but Bob makes mistakes less often. The table below summarizes how a consultant will report under each scenario.

Investment quality Good ({{prior_g}} out of 100 times) Bad ({{prior_b}} out of 100 times)
Bob's report {{high_acc}}% of the times: "The investment is Good." {{high_acc}}% of the times: "The investment is Bad."
{{high_err}}% of the times: "The investment is Bad." {{high_err}}% of the times: "The investment is Good."
Mike's report {{low_acc}}% of the times: "The investment is Good." {{low_acc}}% of the times: "The investment is Bad."
{{low_err}}% of the times: "The investment is Bad." {{low_err}}% of the times: "The investment is Good."

To understand this table, let's take the two cells corresponding to "Good ({{prior_g}} out of 100 times)" and "Bob's report" as an example. These two cells say that if the investment is indeed good, then Bob will say "The investment is Good" {{high_acc}}% of the times, but will mistakenly say "The investment is Bad" {{high_err}}% of the times. The other cells should be interpreted similarly.

After the consultant makes his report, you will have to make a decision. You can either take the investment opportunity, or you can take a lottery that gives you the bonus of {{Constants.high_bonus}} with a certain chance and no bonus otherwise. After you make your decision, we will reveal the quality of the investment as well as the result of the lottery and you will be paid accordingly.
